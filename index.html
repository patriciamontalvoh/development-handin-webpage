Overview

Through this assignment, a functional aggregator interface was created using React. The interface created consists of an online art platform , where users can discover, filter and sort different types of paintings. To begin the assignment, a competitive analysis between competitor companies was conducted in order to analyze strengths and weaknesses in their design elements. From said analysis, the design factors to implement where decided and integrated into the designed interface. The final front end development offers an intuitive user experience with a responsive navigation and a clear layout to emphasizes the artwork.

Part 2: Functionality

Theme: Art online platform

Intention: Intuitive and easy way to discover, filter and sort different types of paintings. Additionally, paintings can be added, moved and removed from the user's Cart and Wishlist.

Incorporated features:

• Filtering categories (which can be added simultaneously):

o By medium (Oil / Acrylic / Pencil)

o By material (Canvas / Board / Paper)

o By author (Miriam Dema / Bea Aigualbella / Klas Ernflo / Marcos Isomat / Naoki Kawano)

• Sorting categories (which cannot be added simultaneously, if one is applied the other one cannot be):

o By price (High to Low / Low to High)

o By size (Large to Small / Small to Large)

• Aggregators

o Cart Items (Add to Cart / Remove from Cart / Move from Wishlist to Cart)

o Wishlist Items (Add to Wishlist / Remove from Wishlist / Move from Cart to Wishlist)

o Removals (Clear all (filters and sorting) / Clear Cart / Clear Wishlist)

• Notifications and labels to keep track of status
